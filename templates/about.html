About Our Hospital

We are a leading provider of healthcare services in our community, offering a wide range of medical specialties and cutting-edge treatments to help our patients achieve their best possible health outcomes.

At our hospital, we are committed to providing the highest level of care to our patients, with a focus on clinical excellence, patient safety, and personalized attention. Our team of doctors, nurses, and support staff work together to ensure that every patient receives the care they need, when they need it.

Our History

Our hospital was founded in 1950 by Dr. John Bhaskaran, who saw the need for a dedicated healthcare facility in our community. Over the years, we have grown and expanded our services to meet the changing needs of our patients, while always staying true to our mission of providing high-quality, compassionate care.

Our Achievements

  • Recipient of the 2020 Healthgrades Patient Safety Excellence Award
  • Named one of the Top Hospitals in the State by KERALA Medical Board
  • Designated as a Comprehensive Stroke Center by the Joint Commission
  • Received the 2021 Best Co-operative hospital Award
