Emergency Department and Services

Our hospital's emergency department is staffed by highly trained medical professionals who are available around the clock to provide immediate medical attention to patients in critical condition. We offer a range of emergency services, including:

  • Triage and assessment of patients to determine the severity of their condition
  • Advanced cardiac life support for patients experiencing heart attacks and other cardiac emergencies
  • Respiratory support for patients experiencing breathing difficulties
  • Access to a blood bank and pharmacy to quickly provide necessary medications and transfusions
  • Advanced imaging and diagnostic services, including X-rays, CT scans, and MRIs
  • Emergency helipad for rapid transport of critically ill patients to and from our hospital

If you or a loved one requires emergency medical attention, please do not hesitate to visit our hospital's emergency department. Our team is dedicated to providing immediate and effective care to patients in their time of need.

Emergency Contact Numbers:

  • Emergency Department: 911
  • Emergency Medical Services: 102
  • Poison Control Center: 1800-222-1222
  • Snakebite Helpline: 1800-180-1104

In case of any emergency, please call the appropriate number above immediately. Our hospital's emergency department is also available 24/7 and can provide immediate assistance.

Hospital Facilities

At our hospital, we are committed to providing our patients with the best possible care in a comfortable and welcoming environment. Our facilities include:

  • 24/7 Emergency Department with state-of-the-art equipment and highly trained medical professionals
  • Fully equipped Intensive Care Unit (ICU)
  • Advanced diagnostic services including X-rays, CT scans, and MRI
  • On-site laboratory services for quick and accurate test results
  • Pharmacy with a wide range of medications and experienced pharmacists
  • Outpatient clinics for a variety of medical specialties
  • Private patient rooms with modern amenities for comfort and convenience
  • Cafeteria serving healthy and delicious meals for patients, visitors, and staff
  • Free Wi-Fi throughout the hospital
  • Parking facilities with ample space for patients and visitors

Our hospital is constantly investing in new technologies and services to improve patient outcomes and provide the highest level of care possible. We are committed to ensuring that our facilities meet the needs of our patients and their families in every way possible.

About Us

Co-operative Hospital was founded in 1985 with the goal of providing accessible and affordable healthcare to the local community. Over the years, we have grown to become one of the most respected and trusted hospitals in the region, with a team of more than 500 dedicated healthcare professionals.
